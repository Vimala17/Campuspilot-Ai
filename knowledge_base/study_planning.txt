If a student misses a class, they should first collect notes from classmates, ask the teacher about important topics, watch one related lecture, and revise the topic the same day.

Catch-up plan:
Day 1: Collect notes and understand main topic
Day 2: Watch lecture or read textbook
Day 3: Solve questions or MCQs
Day 4: Revise and explain the topic in own words

Students should not wait until exams to cover missed topics.
