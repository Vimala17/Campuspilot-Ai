Placement preparation should include aptitude, programming, DSA, resume, projects, communication, and mock interviews.

A 30-day placement plan:
Week 1: Python basics, aptitude, resume improvement
Week 2: DSA basics like arrays, strings, linked lists, stacks, queues
Week 3: SQL, Git, project explanation, HR questions
Week 4: Mock interviews, company research, revision, confidence building

Students should practice coding daily and revise projects clearly.
