Students should manage weekly money by dividing it into food, travel, emergency, savings, and optional spending.

For a small weekly budget like ₹500, students can use:
Food: ₹250
Travel: ₹100
Emergency: ₹50
Savings: ₹50
Extra: ₹50

Students should avoid unnecessary snacks, online orders, impulse shopping, and unused subscriptions.
Tracking expenses daily helps students understand where money is going.
