If a laptop is slow, common reasons include low storage, too many startup apps, temporary files, background apps, outdated Windows, malware, or low RAM.

Solutions:
Restart the laptop
Delete temporary files
Uninstall unused apps
Disable startup apps
Keep at least 20 percent storage free
Update Windows
Run antivirus scan
Close unnecessary browser tabs

If the laptop overheats or shuts down, clean vents and use a cooling pad.
