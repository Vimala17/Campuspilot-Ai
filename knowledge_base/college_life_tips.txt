A student should maintain a balance between academics, extracurricular activities, and personal life.

College Life Tips:
Attend classes regularly
Participate in clubs and events
Build good friendships
Respect teachers and classmates
Keep your notes organized
Avoid last-minute assignments
Take care of your health
Manage your time effectively

Good communication and networking in college can help during placements and internships.

Enjoy college life but never ignore your academic responsibilities.
